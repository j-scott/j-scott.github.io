James Scott

Works    Online Projects    About    






James stages quasi-scientific investigations, with the resulting, often futile experiments, working collectively to navigate a surreal space. A playful attitude towards making emulates notions of the pioneer through to trickster as he dissects everyday systems to produce a type of alchemy through the manipulation of existing structures.
